How this is calculated

  • WTW = Well-to-Wheel. TOC = Transport Operation Category. Intensities are kg CO2e per tonne-km.
  • Baseline assumes all activity is Road diesel at 0.116 (a real demo TOC). At the demo default 16,920 tkm this gives 1,962.72 kg, matching the demo's reported WTW emissions.
  • Rail intensity 0.007 is real (demo TOC toc-rail-1). Electric-road 0.030 and the load-factor effect are illustrative placeholders, not measured TOCs.
  • Scenario = railShare×0.007 + roadShare×(elec×0.030 + (1−elec)×0.116)×(1−loadGain).
